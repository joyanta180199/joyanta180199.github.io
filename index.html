Projects I am working with:

  • BRACU Duburi

    “BRACU Duburi” is the first autonomous underwater vehicle of Bangladesh and was launched in 2017. “Duburi” team was successful to secure 7th position in SAUVC (Singapore Autonomous Underwater Vehicle Competition) 2018.

    The primary aim is to turn up at international competitions with the latest version of “Duburi” consists of newly redesigned mechanical parts and electronics boards allowing the robot to do more than ever before. It is capable of Underwater autonomous navigation, Machine Learning infused underwater computer vision, Underwater ultrasonic localization. To achieve what “Duburi” team set out to do, they have also been busy designing and beginning the creation of their next robot.
